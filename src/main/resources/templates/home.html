autoPost welcomes You

Hi there, I'm autoPost and I'll assist You scheduling the spread of Your words!

Scheduling, Imports and much more

autoPost is a small and easy-to-use webapplication to ease the scheduled publication of Your Posts on a Facebook page.

If You're a power-user, upload a bunch of posts from Your desktop or GoogleDrive: Just follow the markup-guidlines describing the creation of a Post-table or clone the predefined GoogleDrive Spreadsheet, input Your data and import the file - et voilà!

And of course: Much more!
