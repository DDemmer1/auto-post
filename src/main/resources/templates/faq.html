FAQ

What is autoPost?

AutoPost is a web service, where you can write Facebook posts for Facebook pages in advance. You choose the date and the time when autoPost should post your input to your Facebook page.

Can I post as my personal Facebook with autoPost?

No. Facebook shutdown the access to personal Facebook feeds for third party apps. Only Facebook pages can be accessed.
