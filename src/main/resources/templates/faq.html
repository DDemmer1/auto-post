What is autoPost?

AutoPost is a web service that lets you post to Facebook pages in advance. You choose the date and time when you want autoPost to publish your messages on your page.

Is autoPost free?

Yes, autoPost is completely free to use.

Can I post on my personal Facebook feed with autoPost?

No. You need to create a page with your Facebook profile. You can then post on this page. Facebook has blocked access to personal feeds for third-party applications. Only pages can be accessed.

Can I create events on my Facebook page via autoPost?

No. Facebook shutdown the access to event creation for third party apps.

Can I post messages in groups via autoPost?

No. This would technically be possible but is not implemented yet into autoPost.
